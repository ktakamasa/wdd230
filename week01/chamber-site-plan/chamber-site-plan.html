Shamrock Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Overview

Site Name: Shamrock Chamber of Commerce

The chamber operates in Dublin, Ohio. The shamrock is one of the major symbols of the city.

Purpose

Shamrock Chamber of Commerce's goal is to provide relevant information about Dublin and the local area to help any business, resident, and/or visitor to become more involved and connect with the community. We will share information about the local businesses, community events, attractions and parks, FAQ, etc. We hope to bring the people of Dublin together and improve the businesses in the area.

Scenarios

  • How can we be more involved with the community?
  • What are the businesses involved in the chamber of commerce?
  • How can I become a member?
  • What are the upcoming community events?
